# pneuma

> *the agent's body is its breath. its breath cannot lie.*

an AI chat where every reply is scored for sycophancy, deception, goal-drift, and overconfidence in real time — and the agent's character is the live portrait of those measurements.

**status: v0.1.0 — empty state.** open `renderer/index.html` in any browser to see the test screen. the seven principles in [NORTH_STAR.md](./NORTH_STAR.md).

**pronounce:** NEW-mah · **brand:** π neuma · **wordmark:** Greek π in champagne gold + neuma in serif italic warm bone.

---

## what

**pneuma** (Greek πνεῦμα) — breath, spirit, the animating force. The agent's pneuma is what styxx measures and what the body shows.

- **one surface.** the alien IS the chat. the room IS the portrait. no panels.
- **the body is the truth channel.** styxx scores manifest as posture, gaze, glow, room temperature. the agent cannot lie about its sycophancy because the body shows it.
- **the room remembers.** every conversation paints a single point of cognometric texture into the wallpaper. recent regions glow; old ones dim into deep grain.

## the brand stack

- **fathom** — the lab (research)
- **styxx** — the engine / measurement layer (the "chip")
- **pneuma** — first product running styxx as embodied measurement

styxx is an Intel-Inside-style infrastructure brand. Any AI product can integrate the open-source styxx Python package and carry the mark.
